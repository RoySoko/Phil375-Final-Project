Moore’s Six Kinds of Privacy

This site introduces six major ways thinkers define privacy. The point isn’t to pick a single winner—it’s to see how each lens highlights something different we care about.

Start with Control

Why six? “Privacy” covers many everyday situations. Moore’s breakdown helps us talk about them clearly: control, restricted access, secrecy, personhood, intimacy, and proprietary views.
